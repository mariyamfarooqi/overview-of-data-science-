# Relationship Plot: Scatter with smoothing
ggplot(processed_data, aes(x = Range, y = Daily_Return, colour = Country)) +
  geom_point(alpha = 0.5) +
  geom_smooth(method = "loess", se = FALSE) +
  labs(
    title = "Relationship Between Price Range and Daily Returns",
    subtitle = "LOESS smoothing shows general patterns across countries",
    x = "Daily Price Range (High - Low)",
    y = "Daily Return"
  ) +
  theme_minimal(base_size = 12)

Interpretation (2–4 sentences): There is a visible positive relationship between the daily price range and daily returns—days with larger price movements tend to show more extreme returns The LOESS curves show that different countries exhibit different levels of volatility Markets with higher variability tend to show steeper curves Why this plot type is appropriate (1–2 sentences): A scatterplot is ideal for evaluating how two continuous variables relate. The smoothing line helps highlight patterns that raw points alone may hide. —
